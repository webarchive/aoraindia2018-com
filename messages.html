Welcome Message

Message from Chairman

Dr. Balavenkat
National Chairman AORA India.

Dear Colleagues,

Greetings

It would be a pleasure to have you with us for the annual national conference of Academy of Regional Anaesthesia of India scheduled to be held in August in one of the must see places in India, the Kovalam.

One of the most important ingredient of the current anaesthesia practise is to incorporate Regional Anaesthesia as apart of every surgery. The benefits seem to outweigh the risks. Hence it is prudent that every anaesthesiologists have to equip themselves with the various facets of Regional Anaesthesia. The primary objective of AORA India is to promote safe practise of Regional Anaesthesia. AORA India 2018 will offer the delegates the best learning experience in Regional anaesthesia.

The team led by Dr Sajeesh is all set to give us a very memorable time.

Kindly be with us and enrich the meeting.

Looking forward to welcoming you all in God's own country Kerala.

Message from Secretary

Dr. Vrushali Ponde
AORA National Secretary

Dear Friends,

A revered Namaste!

On behalf of the Academy of Regional Anaesthesia of India, it is my pleasure to invite all accomplished anaesthesiologists, budding students, academicians and young researchers, from all over country, and the world, to attend the Eighth National Conference of Academy of Regional Anaesthesia. This focused and dedicated conference shall be held on August 10, 11 & 12, 2018, in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala.

Over the years, we were fortunate to have steadfast commitment from our delegates, a feature we treasure immensely.

Since this conference covers very detailed aspects on regional anaesthesia from fundamental issues to practical applications, anyone interested in future and current progress should not miss this event. AORA conferences are known to share insights into the recent research and cutting-edge technologies, which generates interest with the exuberant presence of adept and brilliant faculty.

Workshops remain the most sought-after aspect of this event, where delegates drop all guards and ask every question they wanted to ask and get answers in return.

We’re looking forward to an excellent meeting with great faculty from different countries around the world.

But our strength lies in the delegates who are so committed to the cause of progress with the belief that patient care improves!

Message from The Academic Director

Dr. Sandeep M. Diwan
Academic Director - AORA India

Dear Colleagues,

It gives me great pleasure extending an invitation to you all for the academic extravaganza at AORA 2018 in Trivandrum. AORA grows stronger by the year thanks to stellar contributions by regional anesthesia enthusiasts. It has been the mission of AORA to equip anaesthesiologists in India with all the latest advances in this expanding field.

In an attempt to reach the remotest of areas, AORA faculties are conducting workshops, CME’s and conferences throughout the length and breadth of the country, thereby generating interest among a wider cross section of anaesthesiologists, especially those working with limited resources. A highlight without doubt over the last two years were the cadaveric workshops.

This year the academic sessions are planned to meet the needs of both the novice and the expert. A balance between the basics and advances will keep the delegates engrossed for two days.

Renowned international faculties, Jeff Gadsden, Phillipe Gautier and Nicholas Scott are going to make their mark in AORA 2018. It would be great to hear their original contributions. The national faculties include a blend from the major centres and remote locations in India.

As the D day approaches, I urge all to register for AORA 2018 conference and experience the endearing hospitality of the local organizing committee.
