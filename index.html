WELCOME TO AORA THIRUVANANTHAPURAM 2018

Anaesthetic practice hold an unparalleled role in making medicine a modern scientific discourse and has become the cutting edge in its precision and safety. Regional anaesthesia is growing as an integral component of anaestheticpractice and it has improvised through ultrasound guided nerve blocks in recent years. The foundation of AORA has set its goal to empower all practicing anaesthesiologists with knowledge and skills of advancements in regional anaesthesia.

The 8th National Conference shall provide scientific sessions of lectures, panel discussions, seminars, workshops and video demonstrations by national and international faculties of regional anaesthesia. There will be live video telecasts with direct interaction with the experts demonstrating regional blocks. The conference has pivotal role inmoulding the budding anaesthesiologists. The event includes free paper presentations, poster presentations for post graduate students. There will be opportunity to win exciting prizes as well. We look forward to see you at Trivandrum enjoying the exciting event of AORA India, from 10th to 12th of August 2018.
